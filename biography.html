Biography

I am a PhD student in Industrial Engineering at the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering at Georgia Tech. My research focuses on optimization methods for statistical learning, with an emphasis on scalable mixed integer programming formulations.

Before joining Georgia Tech, I completed my B.Tech. in Production and Industrial Engineering and M.Tech. in Industrial Engineering at IIT Delhi.
